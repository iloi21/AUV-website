Titan Underwater Robotics AUV

About us

At the Titan Underwater Robotics Club, our mission is to unite curious minds passionate about underwater robotics. Whether you're a seasoned builder or just starting to explore the field, we welcome your ideas and invite you to dive deep into discovery, collaboration, and innovation. This club is all about:

  • Building collaborative underwater robotics projects
  • Exploring cutting-edge technologies and engineering principles
  • Sharing knowledge, resources, and expertise in AUV development
  • Engaging in real-world applications, competitions, and research opportunities.
